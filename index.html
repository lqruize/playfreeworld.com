About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival game that puts your skills to the test. As night falls, waves of terrifying monsters emerge from the darkness. Your mission is to survive as long as possible while collecting resources and upgrading your abilities.

Game Features

  • Intense wave-based survival gameplay
  • Multiple character classes to choose from
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Upgradeable abilities and weapons
  • Engaging monster variety

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Click or press space to attack. Collect experience points to level up and choose new abilities. Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters.
